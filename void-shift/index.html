    ⚡ HOW TO PLAY
    VOID SHIFT — MECHANICS GUIDE
    🎯 GOAL
    Score as high as you can before the 8×8 grid fills up completely. New cells spawn every few seconds — keep the board clear by merging!
    🖱️ CONTROLS
    1. Click a cell → it becomes selected (white ring)
    2a. Click same type → cells MERGE → evolve to next type + score!
    2b. Click different type → creates a PUSH FORCE → the 2nd cell gets pushed away
    Click empty space → deselects
    ⚡ FORCE LINES
    You can have up to 5 active force lines at once (more as you level up).
    Use forces to push cells out of the way when you can't merge yet.
    Click the ✕ next to a force to remove it, or use CLEAR ALL.
    🔥 COMBOS
    Chain multiple merges in a row for a combo multiplier!
    3+ combo gives bonus points. Combos reset when a new game starts.
    🧬 EVOLUTION CHAIN (12 stages)
    ✨SPARK→ 🔥FLAME→ ⚡PLASMA→ 💧WATER→ ❄️ICE→ 🌀STORM→ ⚫VOID→ 🌿LIFE→ 🌳NATURE→ 💎CRYSTAL→ 🌑DARK→ 🌟COSMOS
    Each merge gives points × level. Reaching COSMOS = 5000 × level bonus explosion!
    💡 TIPS
    • Group same-type cells near each other so they chain-merge automatically
    • Use force lines to cluster cells before the grid fills up
    • Higher-tier merges (VOID, LIFE, COSMOS) give massive points
    • Cells auto chain-merge if two of the same type end up adjacent after a merge!
